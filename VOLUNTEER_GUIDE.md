## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### QR Code Won't Scan?
1. Ask attendee to increase screen brightness
2. Clean your camera lens
3. Try from a different angle
4. Move to a shaded area (reduce glare)

### Page Not Loading?
1. Check your internet connection
2. Wait a few seconds and try again
3. Switch to mobile data if WiFi is slow

### Already Used Error?
- Ticket has already been scanned for entry
- **DO NOT** allow entry again
- Politely explain the ticket was already used
- Direct them to the supervisor if they have concerns

### Invalid Ticket?
- Ticket ID not found in system
- Could be a fake ticket
- **DO NOT** allow entry
- Call a supervisor immediately
